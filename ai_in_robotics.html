Robotics Overview

Robotics is the branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, operation, and application of robots. Robots are programmed to perform tasks that are usually done by humans or to do things that are dangerous or difficult for humans to do.

AI in Robotics

AI plays a crucial role in robotics by enabling robots to perceive their environment, make decisions, and perform tasks autonomously. Machine learning algorithms allow robots to learn from experience and improve their performance over time.

Applications of AI in Robotics

AI-powered robots are used in various applications such as manufacturing (assembly line robots), healthcare (surgical robots), agriculture (autonomous tractors), space exploration (rover robots), and more.
